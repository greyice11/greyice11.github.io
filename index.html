About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), South Korea, specializing in Construction Engineering and Project Management. My research focuses on developing AI-powered systems for construction automation and safety.

My work spans deep learning for defect detection, vision-language models for safety guidance, and autonomous robotic inspection systems. I integrate Digital Twin technologies with real-time sensing for predictive maintenance and quality management.

Deep Learning

PyTorch, TensorFlow, YOLO, Detectron2

Research Interests

Construction AI

Vision-Language Models

Autonomous Systems

Building robotic platforms with SLAM and navigation for automated inspection and monitoring.

Digital Twins

Integrating BIM with real-time sensor data for predictive maintenance in smart facilities.
